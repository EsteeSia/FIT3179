Annual Maternal Deaths by State in Malaysia

January 2000 to January 2022

Relationship between Maternal Deaths and Mortality Rate

This scatterplot illustrates the correlation between the number of maternal deaths and the maternal mortality rate across different states in Malaysia. Each point represents a state, with its position indicating both the number of deaths and the corresponding mortality rate. The visualization helps identify patterns and potential outliers in maternal health outcomes across the country.

Observations from this chart can guide policymakers in targeting healthcare interventions to states with higher mortality rates or numbers of deaths.

Maternal Mortality Rate by State

This treemap provides a hierarchical view of maternal mortality rates across Malaysian states. The size of each rectangle corresponds to the mortality rate in that state, offering a quick visual comparison of maternal health outcomes between different regions.

This visualization is particularly useful for identifying states that may require more focused healthcare interventions or additional resources to improve maternal health outcomes.
